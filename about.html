Echoes of Celebration

This website was born from a desire to preserve and share the heartbeat of Nigerian culture, its festivals. Each celebration tells a story of joy, unity, ancestors, and identity. Yet many of these stories fade in digital silence.

By bringing these festivals into a virtual space, this project becomes a bridge between generations, between tradition and innovation. It imagines a future where technology does not erase heritage, but amplifies it.

In the world I hope for, culture thrives in both the physical and digital realms accessible, beautiful, and deeply human. This is a space where we listen to the rhythm of drums through pixels and remember who we are.

Author: Gbemisayo Adelaja

Course: Web-Based Multimedia Design
